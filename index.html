About Bagodih

Bagodih is a beautiful village located in Tehsil Jainagar. Known for its natural beauty, culture, and peaceful life, it's home to kind-hearted people and a rich heritage.“People in Bagodih mind their own business and are happy in their own lives. Festivals are celebrated together with unity and joy. Everyone goes to their fields, plants paddy, and grows seasonal crops. The people of Bagodih are hardworking and live a simple yet fulfilling life.”
